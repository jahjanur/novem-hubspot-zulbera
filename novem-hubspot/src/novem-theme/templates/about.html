Why We Built Novem Digital

Risk was invisible.
We made it predictable.

"Failure is Predictable." That is not a tagline. It is what the data kept telling us — until we built a platform to prove it at scale.

We discovered the problem firsthand while bridging construction, commissioning, and operational data on large commercial projects. Handovers took years. Costs were hidden. Risk was invisible. And ultimately, the owner absorbed all of it.

We used data to change that. By connecting real-time building signals with historical loss and claims data from millions of buildings, we found we could predict failures before they became emergencies.

Occupancy permits came faster. Emergency spend dropped. Insurance outcomes improved. Every stakeholder saved money.

That insight became Novem Digital — the only Digital Risk Platform combining continuous monitoring with over twenty years of insurance and equipment data. Our customers reduce Total Cost of Risk Ownership, protect cash flow, and defend every capital and insurance decision with audit-ready data.

Because when risk stays invisible, it is not just balance sheets that take the hit.
