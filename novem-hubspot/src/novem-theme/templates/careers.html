How We Work

The principles that
drive everything.

Conviction over consensus
We'd rather be right and argued with than wrong and agreed with. Every decision is made on first principles, not precedent.
Speed with substance
We move fast — but we ship things that hold up. Urgency and quality are not opposites here.
Radical transparency
Everyone knows the numbers, the strategy, and the hard conversations. No information silos, no surprises.
Small team, full ownership
You own your domain. There are no layers between your decision and its impact on the product and the customer.
Global from day one
We don't think locally. Our customers are institutional, our ambitions are global, and our team reflects that.
Mission over metrics
Every KPI we track traces back to a building that performed better. We don't optimize vanity — we optimize outcomes.
