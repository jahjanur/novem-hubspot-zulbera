FAQ

Common questions.

What types of resources does Novem Digital publish?

We publish case studies documenting real outcomes across commercial real estate, seniors living, data centers, and public venues — plus white papers, blog perspectives, and press releases. Case studies open in a dedicated page with full data, methodology, and results.

What is the TCRO Framework whitepaper?

The TCRO Framework whitepaper defines Total Cost of Risk Ownership — the financial standard that captures everything traditional Cost of Risk misses. It gives finance and operations leaders a shared language for defending capital allocation and insurance decisions with audit-ready data.

How can I access the full case studies?

Each case study card opens the full study in a new page — including challenge, solution, pullquotes, and detailed results tables. No form required. Click any "Read case study" link to open it.

When will white papers and blog content be published?

White papers, blog posts, and press releases are currently in development. Content is published as it is cleared for release. Get in touch if you'd like early access to specific topics — we're happy to share what's in the pipeline.
