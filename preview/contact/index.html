Common Questions

Before you message us.

The most-asked questions before a demo — answered up front.

How long does a demo take?

Thirty minutes. We walk through the platform on your asset class, show how TCRO surfaces, and answer your questions live. No slide-only deck.

Do you need our data to demo?

No. We start with our sample portfolio. If you want to see your data, we can layer it in during a second session under a one-page NDA.

What asset classes do you cover?

Commercial & mixed-use, seniors & wellness, data centers, and recreational & venues today. Custom asset classes on request.

Do you integrate with our existing tools?

Yes — BMS, IoT, CMMS, insurance, and ERP systems. We handle ingestion; you don't need to rebuild your stack.

How does pricing work?

Annual subscription, scaled by portfolio size and modules deployed. We'll share a tailored quote after the first demo.

Where is my data stored?

SOC 2-aligned cloud infrastructure in the region of your choice (North America or EU). Data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
