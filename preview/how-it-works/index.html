Common Questions

Questions worth answering directly.

What is a digital risk platform for real estate?

A digital risk platform connects live building data, risk intelligence, and financial exposure in one system so owners can predict and prevent failures before they become loss events. Novem is the only platform that combines real-time monitoring with normalized claims data from 7.2 million buildings.

What is Total Cost of Risk Ownership?

TCRO is the full financial burden of invisible building risk — direct claims, emergency repair premiums, insurance volatility, business interruption, and compliance overhead. Most organizations only count direct claims. Novem reduces the full spectrum.

How is Novem different from a building management system?

A BMS monitors and controls in real time. Novem layers predictive intelligence and 20 years of claims data on top — so operational signals become capital planning, governance, and insurance decisions. Novem doesn't replace your BMS. It makes its data financially useful.

Does Novem require replacing existing systems?

No. Novem connects to what you already own, then adds targeted IoT coverage only where financial exposure is highest. One credible digital risk record — no rip-and-replace required.

How does Novem help with insurance renewals?

Novem produces continuous, audit-ready data that demonstrates proactive risk management to underwriters. Instead of defending a submission with historical claims alone, Novem customers go to renewal with documented evidence of monitoring, early intervention, and measurable reduction.
