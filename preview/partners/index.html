FAQ

Common questions.

What types of organizations does Novem partner with?
Novem works with insurers, brokerages, building technology vendors, sensor and data providers, and real estate service firms. If your business touches building risk in any form, there's likely a partnership model that works.
Is the platform open to third-party integrations?
Yes. Novem is built on open protocols by design. It connects with any building automation system, sensor vendor, or data feed — no proprietary lock-in. We actively support partner integrations through documented APIs.
Does Novem operate internationally?
Yes. Novem serves institutional portfolios across Canada, the United States, and Europe. We actively seek partners with global reach who want to extend predictive risk intelligence to their international clients.
