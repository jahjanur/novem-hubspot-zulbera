Brand New Building.
Hidden Failures.

The Facility

New equipment.
Assumed to work.

Bethany Riverview is a four-storey seniors living facility in Calgary, Alberta, Canada — 200,000 square feet of space above a 45,000 square foot underground parkade. The $53M purpose-built facility houses 210 residents including complex dementia care, long-term care, and bariatric care rooms.

With brand new equipment and systems, the team assumed commissioning would be straightforward. What Novem found told a different story.

Equipment that had never been properly set up. Systems failing silently. Wasted energy from day one. The failures weren't visible through inspection — they required continuous data analysis to detect.

Novem's platform caught it all before any of it became a maintenance crisis or an insurance event.

What Novem Found

Every failure.
Before move-in.

Air Handling Units — Not Operational
Fan drives had not been properly commissioned and were unable to run. Without positive pressurization, cold outside air migrated into the facility — a direct risk to residents and a comfort failure across every floor.
Rooftop HVAC — Not Responding to Heating Calls
Analytics detected rooftop HVAC units failing to respond correctly. Contractors attended site with digital forensics in hand — issues resolved same visit.
Dirty Air Filters — Driving Energy Waste
Pressure drop analytics flagged filters running at elevated resistance. Variable speed fans compensating at higher speeds. Filters replaced. Energy waste stopped.
Heat Exchanger — Delivering No Heat
Manual isolation valves were closed. Circulation pumps overridden to off at the local control panel. Heating performance restored immediately on correction.

“Bethany’s implementation validated the value of the data for insurance, owners, and construction teams. Every issue caught before handover is a liability that never makes it onto the risk register.”

— Novem Digital, Bethany Riverview Case Study
