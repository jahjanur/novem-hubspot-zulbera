Novem Digital Appoints Grant R. Jay as Chief Revenue Officer

Strategic GTM leader with 15+ years driving enterprise growth across deep tech, critical infrastructure, and AI joins to lead global commercial expansion.

VANCOUVER, BC, April 21, 2026 — Novem Digital, the predictive intelligence platform purpose-built for institutional real estate portfolios, today announced the appointment of Grant R. Jay as Chief Revenue Officer. Jay brings more than 15 years of enterprise business development and GTM leadership across some of the most technically demanding and regulated industries in the world — from smart buildings and digital twins to satellite intelligence, critical infrastructure, and agentic AI platforms.

As Chief Revenue Officer, Jay will lead Novem Digital's global commercial strategy — driving enterprise sales, partner ecosystems, and market expansion across Canada, the United States, and Europe.

"Predictive intelligence is the next frontier for institutional operators — and Novem is positioned to lead it. I've spent 15 years helping enterprise organizations recognize that the shift from reactive to predictive isn't a technology decision, it's a revenue and risk decision. I'm here to make that conversation happen at scale." — Grant R. Jay, Chief Revenue Officer, Novem Digital

Built for Complex Enterprise Sales

Jay's career spans industries where long sales cycles, multi-stakeholder decisions, and technical credibility are table stakes. He has driven enterprise revenue at DigitalGlobe (now Maxar Technologies), where he opened the first commercially sold cloud GIS services contract in Canadian history with the Government of British Columbia. He led the Minerals & Mining division across Canada for one of the world's largest satellite imagery companies, building executive-level partnerships with government, academia, and industry bodies from NRCan to PDAC.

At Minerva Intelligence — acquired by Seequent-Bentley Systems — Jay served as VP Growth and Partnerships, driving revenue across mining, ESG, climate risk, wildfire, and flood intelligence. At Shiftboard, he led North American sales into oil and gas, utilities, healthcare, corrections, and manufacturing, achieving 145% quota in his final year and earning the Leadership Sales Excellence Award.

His track record reflects an uncommon ability to operate at the intersection where technical solutions meet enterprise procurement — delivering strategic partnership programs, paid proof-of-concept frameworks, and long-term contracted agreements across some of the most complex customer environments in North America.

Why Jay — and Why Now

Novem Digital is entering its global growth phase. The platform's ability to predict operational failure across commercial real estate, seniors living, data centers, and public venues is proven. What this moment demands is the commercial architecture to match — the enterprise relationships, the partner ecosystems, and the GTM discipline to convert that proof into global market leadership.

Jay's appointment accelerates three commercial priorities:

  • Enterprise Revenue Leadership — C-suite engagement and long-cycle deal execution with institutional portfolio operators, insurance partners, and infrastructure owners
  • Global Partner Ecosystems — strategic and preferred partnership programs that extend Novem's reach across Canada, the U.S., and Europe
  • Agentic AI GTM — positioning Novem's platform within the rapidly evolving landscape of AI-driven operations and enterprise decision workflows

Jay's deep fluency across smart buildings, digital twins, SCADA and telemetry, OT environments, and critical infrastructure means he enters every enterprise conversation with the operational vocabulary that builds trust — and closes deals. His experience with Bell Canada (Bell Presidents Club, 169% of quota), Motorola Solutions (Motorola Leadership Circle), and DigitalGlobe (Key Deals Award) reflects consistent elite performance in complex, relationship-driven sales environments.

"Grant brings exactly what this stage requires: the ability to walk into a boardroom, speak the language of operators and institutional investors, and translate the Novem value proposition into a decision. His track record across critical infrastructure, smart buildings, and emerging AI platforms is precisely what our commercial expansion demands." — Novem Digital Executive Leadership
